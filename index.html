Intro

Hi my name is Patrick La. I am an Australian born Chinese that is currently studying Bachelor of Information Technology at RMIT University. I graduated high school in 2013, after graduating high school I got accepted into Bachelor of Computer and Network Systems at Victoria University. After half a year of the course I got too lazy and decided to defer from the course. During those years in between I ended up working for a bit with my father as we own a family business. It wasn’t until 2018 that I decided to come back to studying because of all the nagging from my parents. So here I am a 25-year-old student looking to complete my university degree and find a job after graduating. One of my hobbies is that I enjoy eating. I am a big foodie and used to regularly visit brunch spots weekly and post it on Instagram. Fun fact is that I have completed a 2kg Japanese curry challenge before. Another hobby is that I enjoy playing video games. I have always been a very competitive person at a very young age I was introduced to competitive shooter games such as counter strike, I have been playing since the 1.5 – 1.6 days then I moved to other shooter games such as Combat Arms and Call of Duty where I was a leader of a clan and would recruit other members and versed other clans. During my Highschool years I would attend yearly LAN’s (LAN-slide) for Call of Duty tournaments. awesome work.
